About us

Yüzer&Aydın Law firm is an independent law firm with 30 years of experience that is based in Istanbul/Turkey. We represent domestic and international clients that inclued individuals and companies. We, as a team, consist of three lawyers, three interns and three assistants. We also receive consulting services from four consultants who are experts in their fields. In our law firm we are a family. We work dedicatedly and we’re always in harmony. In our law firm wide expreience from experienced lawyers meets dynamism from young Lawyers. Our law firm attaches great importance to legal consultancy service and aims to provide the best legal consultancy service with its competent and expert professional staff in the field of preventing possible problems of its clients and solving their problems. With the representation service we provide to our clients, we reveal our legal knowledge and experience and serve in order to obtain the most positive results on behalf of our clients. The principle of our office is that the legal service provided is carried out with a quality policy in the light of professionalism. Attorneyship profession requires a mutual trust relationship with the client due to its nature. In this context, the principles of transparency, care, urgency, confidentiality and courtesy constitute are the steps of our professional quality policy during the conduct of the profession. We focus on building relationships. We see our clients as our business partners. It is a fact that for all clients, we aim to build long-standing, cooperative relationships, based on an in-depth understanding of what their strategic goals and success require.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and fast service to our clients with our contemporary, innovative approach to legal service in our country and with our expert lawyers and team. In this context, our law firm aims to be a leading law firm in its sector with an understanding based on continuous development, quality, teamwork, professional management understanding, detailed work, information, use of technology and sharing, dealing with the problems of the clients from the beginning to the conclusion with the establishment of a mutual trust relationship with its clients. While performing the activities of representation and consultancy services to the client; To act within the framework of universal moral, legal values, basic principles and obligations of the legal profession (Independence, Freedom, Confidentiality, Loyalty to the Client, Fairness, Fairness, Self-Control, Respect for the rule of law and fair administration of the judiciary, Reputation and honor of the legal profession and personal honesty and dignity of the lawyer, Continuous learning, Innovation and technology, Sharing, Pioneering, Scientificity, Value Creation, Production, Continuity etc.b.).

Our Vision

Our primary goal is; to grow by developing and in this context, our vision is always to renew. Our law firm, which was established at the point of combining these goals, reveals its difference in the legal community by bringing a solution-oriented approach and system to the problem with our experienced and knowledgeable lawyer staff and our young colleagues who aim to walk on this path with determination.
