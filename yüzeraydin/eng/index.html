Maritime Trade and Transportation Law

The law of the sea is defined as the branch of law that regulates the legal relations that arise between individuals and between individuals and the state and states due to the voyage of ships at sea. Maritime Trade Law is the branch of law that regulates the legal relations arising between individuals due to the navigation of merchant ships at sea.
