About us

Yüzer & Aydın Law Firm is an independent law firm based in Istanbul with 30 years of experience.As Yüzer & Aydın Law Firm, with our deep-rooted staff consisting of lawyers and consultants, each of whom is an expert and experienced in the field, with the countless experiences we have gained since our establishment, we provide legal services and consultancy to our domestic and foreign clients based on fundamental human rights and freedoms, based on the rule of law.With the representation service we offer to our clients, we reveal our legal knowledge and experience and work to get the most positive results on behalf of our clients.The principles of transparency, care, urgency, confidentiality and courtesy constitute the steps of our professional quality policy.

Our Mission

As Yüzer & Aydın Law Firm, our mission is to meet the legal needs of our clients within the framework of social values, respect for human rights and legal principles, in accordance with universal legal norms; with modern and innovative approaches, with our expert lawyers and team, to solve it in a reliable, effective and fast way.

Our Vision

As Yüzer & Aydın Law Firm, our vision is to contribute to the increase of respect and belief in justice and law within the framework of the rule of law, justice principle and confidentiality principles; to provide quality service to our clients by professionally defending the rights and freedoms of our clients by representing them in the best way at home and abroad; to closely follow all developments in our legal system and implement them on behalf of the clients we represent; to work for the realization of justice with an honest, reliable, contemporary, successful vision; To be a distinguished Law Firm that is closely interested in the problems of the country and the world, that can interpret and evaluate the developments on a national and global scale.
